Roll Initiative!

A wild Quizit has appeared!

A Quizit is the (very) weak cousin of the Quasit, a demon from the lower planes. Quizits don't really attack people but instead they do something MUCH worse... give a Pop Quiz!!

Your only hope of survival will be to beat this Quizit's Pop Quiz, lest you be trapped in an endless cycle of multiple choice questions for eternity!
Dun Dun Dunnn...

Are you ready?

Yes! Give me the Pop Quiz. No! I'll read the instructions first.

1. What type of dragon has the most HP?

2. What is a Redcap?

3. What year was Dungeons and Dragons 5th edition released?

4. Select each option that is NOT a real D&D monster

5. Which language is primarily used by Demons?

You think you got enough points to survive? Well then, it is time to test your fate...

  • 1: Ancient Gold Dragon
    Ancient Gold and Red Dragons are tied for the top spot with 546 HP and both are immune to fire damage.
  • 2: A Chaotic Fey
    A Redcap, specifically referring to D&D, are homicidal Feywild creatures born of blood lust (literally).
  • 3: 2014
    I hope you got this one since it says the answer at the top of the page.
  • 4: Flying Sharks
    While they were a menace in Sharknado, Flying Sharks are not an official D&D monster. All the other things are though! Check 'em out.
  • 5: Abyssal
    Abyssal is the primary language of Demons and Infernal is the language of Devils. Demonic and Fiendish are made up.

Did you pass? I hope so, thank you for playing! I hope you had fun learning about Dungeons and Dragons!

Instructions

Due to the nature of this project I have opted to not add in a bunch of JavaScript to properly validate this form. I have instead added only a tiny bit of JavaScript. An answer key will appear upon clicking "Show Answers" for you to see how well you did. I was going to make the submit button do this but since we are required to have a submit button I did not want to break any rules by removing the submit type.

The Quizit has 4 HP and there are 5 questions. With each correct question you do 1 "damage" to the Quizit. So, as long as you get at least 4/5 you've killed the Quizit and you win! If you don't get at least 4/5 the Quizit will trap you in an endless quiz dimension for eternity. Don't say I didn't warn ya. Good Luck!
